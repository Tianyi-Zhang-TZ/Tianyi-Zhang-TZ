About Me ([CV in English] [中文简历])

I am a computer science Ph.D candidate at the Distributed & Interactive Systems Group in Centrum Wiskunde & Informatica (CWI), the national research institute for mathematics and computer science in the Netherlands. I am also associated with the Multimedia Computing Group in Delft University of Technology (TU Delft). I am so fortunate to work toward my PhD under the supervision of Prof. dr. Pablo Cesar (TU Delft, CWI), Dr. Abdallah El Ali (CWI), and Prof. dr. Alan Hanjalic (TU Delft). My research interest includes:

  • Human-Computer Interaction
  • Affective Computing
  • Physiological Sensing
  • Machine Learning and Deep learning
  • Computer Vision and its application
